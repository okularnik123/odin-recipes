Spaghetti

Description

A type of food, light pasta, the basis of Italian cuisine.
In English, note is also used in recipes for dishes with this pasta
A typical dish consists of a portion of fresh pasta mixed with controlled
sauce and optionally sprinkled with cheese.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • Bologneze Sauce
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Onions
  • Tomatos

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted water
  2. Toss the cooked spaghetti with a flavorful sauce
  3. Serve the dish on plates
  4. Enjoy your meal
