Burger

Description

The classic burger is an all-time BBQ favourite!
This super easy homemade beef burger recipe gives you delicious patties, packed with onions and herbs for extra flavour,
that are perfect for topping with cheese, lettuce and tomato, and sandwiching between floury buns

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • burger bun
  • Salat
  • Cucumber
  • Onions
  • Tomatos

Steps

  1. Prepare and fry meat
  2. Make a sauce to a burger
  3. Combine all ingredients
  4. Enjoy your meal
