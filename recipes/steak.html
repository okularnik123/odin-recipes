Steak

Description

This Pan-Seared Steak has a garlic rosemary-infused
butter that makes it taste steakhouse quality.
Youll be impressed at how easy it is to make the perfect
steak seared and caramelized on the outside, and so juicy inside.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Oil
  • Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Other spices

Steps

  1. Combine steak with spices
  2. Fry steak
  3. Serve the dish on plates with sauce
  4. Enjoy your meal
